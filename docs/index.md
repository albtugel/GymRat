# GymRat

A minimalist workout tracker for building programs and logging progress. No accounts, no clutter.

## Highlights

- Weekly timeline with day selection
- Program builder (strength, cardio, crossfit)
- Fast logging for sets, reps, and weights
- Cardio rounds and duration tracking
- Drag & drop ordering
- Works offline with local storage (SwiftData)

## Privacy

We do not collect personal data. Your workouts stay on your device.
