# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 24, 2026

GymRat is a local‑only workout tracker. We do not collect or transmit personal data.

## Data We Collect
None. GymRat does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

## Local Storage
All workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData.

## Data Sharing
We do not share data with third parties.

## Analytics and Tracking
GymRat does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

## Accounts and Internet
No account is required. GymRat works offline.

## Children
GymRat is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes
If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page.
